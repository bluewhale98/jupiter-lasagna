A Reputation for Excellence

We preserve your inheritance rights to Florida real estate.

Each month in Florida, several thousand property owners pass away leaving tens of thousands of heirs to inherited real estate. Many times the legal heirs have no knowledge of their inheritance and are quite difficult to find.

We specialize in finding and advising absentee heirs of their legal inheritance rights to real estate and how they can claim their inheritance quickly without spending a single dime on attorney fees or probate costs.

We offer creative solutions to all situations and have helped thousands of heirs over the years claim lost inheritance money.

We can help you do the same.
